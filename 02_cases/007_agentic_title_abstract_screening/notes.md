# Case 7: Agentic title/abstract screening in migration-policy reviews with gray-literature rescue

## Summary

Test AI-first screening on mixed corpora to see whether first-pass screening shrinks while protocol design and corpus rescue remain human.

## Why it matters for typology

It tests both shrinkage and durable boundary-setting in one workflow.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: AI-first workflows preserve gray and multilingual evidence without human rescue
- Main confounds: Access to subscriptions and topic difficulty can mimic AI effects
- Evidence status: Peer-reviewed article
