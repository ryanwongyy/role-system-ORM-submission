# Case 22: Fine-tuned SocSci210 human-behavior simulators

## Summary

Test whether fine-tuning on past open experiments changes the human role from first-pass prediction to calibration and scope setting.

## Why it matters for typology

It pressures the most plausible transformed role under predictive AI: calibration.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: Fine-tuned models generalize so well that human calibration rarely changes use decisions
- Main confounds: Benchmark overfitting can overstate real screening value
- Evidence status: Preprint / conference paper

## Sources collected

- `C022_P1` (preprint / open): Finetuning LLMs for Human Behavior Prediction in Social Science Experiments — https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.05830
