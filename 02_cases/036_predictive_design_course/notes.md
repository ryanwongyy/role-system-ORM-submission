# Case 36: Methods-course experiment with predictive design tools and provenance logging

## Summary

Randomize doctoral or advanced methods students to standard design training versus predictive-AI-plus-provenance training to see which role distinctions are teachable.

## Why it matters for typology

This is the cleanest way to test whether the typology can structure training rather than only retrospective analysis.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: Students trained mainly in prompting perform just as well on identification and provenance tasks
- Main confounds: Artificial classroom settings may not map to real lab or dissertation pressures
- Evidence status: Proposed case design
