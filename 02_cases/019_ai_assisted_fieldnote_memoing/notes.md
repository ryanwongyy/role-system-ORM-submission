# Case 19: AI-assisted memoing of ethnographic or historical fieldnotes

## Summary

Compare memo trails and final claims produced from the same fieldnote corpus with and without AI assistance.

## Why it matters for typology

It is the best case for making tacit contextual knowledge visible after data collection is over.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: AI-assisted and human-only memo trails converge without loss of contextual subtlety
- Main confounds: Some fieldnotes are highly systematic and easier for AI than live field settings
- Evidence status: Forum article / boundary evidence
