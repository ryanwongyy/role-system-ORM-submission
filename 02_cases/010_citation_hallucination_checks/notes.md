# Case 10: Citation-hallucination checks in AI-assisted literature-review drafts

## Summary

Track when humans verify or fail to verify citations inserted by AI during literature-review drafting and evidence synthesis.

## Why it matters for typology

This case can show whether two typology categories collapse in practice.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: Citation support tools eliminate hallucination risk without extra human verification work
- Main confounds: Poor citation habits could predate AI and inflate apparent AI effects
- Evidence status: Preprint / commentary cluster
