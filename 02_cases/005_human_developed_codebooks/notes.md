# Case 5: Human-developed codebooks for policy stakeholder interview coding

## Summary

Compare AI coding quality when the codebook is supplied by policy experts versus when AI proposes themes and labels first.

## Why it matters for typology

Visible codebook and revision traces make the role shift empirically inspectable.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: AI-first thematic analysis without human codebook development is equally specific and usable
- Main confounds: Policy interviews may be more structured than ethnographic or highly sensitive data
- Evidence status: Peer-reviewed article
