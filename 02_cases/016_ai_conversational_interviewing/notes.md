# Case 16: AI conversational interviewing on welfare stigma or political fear

## Summary

Compare AI-only, human-only, and human+AI interviewing on sensitive topics to test whether rapport, repair, and ethical pause remain constitutive.

## Why it matters for typology

It directly tests whether the human changes the data that come into existence.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: AI-only interviewing matches humans on trust, repair, and interpretive usefulness
- Main confounds: Mode effects depend heavily on topic sensitivity and respondent population
- Evidence status: Preprint

## Sources collected

- `C016_P1` (preprint / open): AI Conversational Interviewing: Transforming Surveys with LLMs as Adaptive Interviewers — https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.01824
