# Case 8: Study-type classification and review triage for social-policy evidence synthesis

## Summary

Benchmark specialized classifiers and LLMs for study-type classification in reviews where social-policy evidence mixes methods and weak metadata.

## Why it matters for typology

It isolates the triage function from broader synthesis claims.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: Predictive triage works equally well on heterogeneous and poorly structured sources
- Main confounds: Benchmark corpora may be cleaner than actual policy-review inputs
- Evidence status: Peer-reviewed article
