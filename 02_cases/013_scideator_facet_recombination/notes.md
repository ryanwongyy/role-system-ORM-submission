# Case 13: Scideator-style facet recombination for social-science prospectus ideation

## Summary

Adapt facet-recombination interfaces to social-science dissertation or proposal ideation and compare human-only with human-LLM compound ideation.

## Why it matters for typology

Useful for separating novelty assistance from substantive relevance judgment.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: Human-LLM ideation does not outperform simple prompting or human-only ideation on meaningful criteria
- Main confounds: Existing Scideator evidence is not social-science-specific and may overstate transfer
- Evidence status: Preprint

## Sources collected

- `C013_P1` (preprint / open): Human-LLM Compound System for Scientific Ideation through Facet Recombination and Novelty Evaluation — https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.14634
- `C013_S1` (preprint / open): Can LLMs Generate Novel Research Ideas? A Large-Scale Human Study with 100+ NLP Researchers — https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.04109
