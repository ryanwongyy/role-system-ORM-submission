# Case 17: Dynamic Surveys with AI-generated follow-ups and cluster reports

## Summary

Use dynamic surveys to test a middle ground between structured surveys and interviews, with AI-generated follow-ups and emergent cluster reports.

## Why it matters for typology

Excellent hybrid case because it pressures the line between interviewing and structured surveying.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: Dynamic surveys produce equivalent depth and usefulness without any human interpretive scaffolding
- Main confounds: Educational settings may not generalize to high-stakes public-policy or vulnerable populations
- Evidence status: Peer-reviewed conference paper
