# Case 1: Objective party-affiliation annotation across 11 countries

## Summary

Use politician social-media posts with known party labels to test when human coders can be demoted from primary coders to auditors.

## Why it matters for typology

Public ground truth makes it one of the cleanest displacement tests available now.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: Humans continue to outperform or materially change downstream conclusions on objective tasks
- Main confounds: Task is unusually objective; may not generalize to contested constructs
- Evidence status: Peer-reviewed article
