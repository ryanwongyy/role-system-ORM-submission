# Case 33: Deep Research agents for policy-literature reconnaissance

## Summary

Adapt long-horizon web-research agents to frozen or live policy-literature tasks and examine when human source-boundary decisions still dominate.

## Why it matters for typology

Useful to stress-test corpus pluralism under live or semi-live web search conditions.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: Agentic research produces equally diverse and well-grounded corpora without human boundary setting
- Main confounds: Current deep-research benchmarks are not social-science-specific and often use frozen web corpora
- Evidence status: Preprint benchmark
