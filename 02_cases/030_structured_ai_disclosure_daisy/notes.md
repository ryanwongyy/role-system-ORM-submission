# Case 30: Structured AI disclosure with DAISY

## Summary

Use DAISY to test whether structured disclosure changes what authors report about AI use and how they experience responsibility for it.

## Why it matters for typology

Good test of whether a role is about reflective accountability or just compliance.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: Structured disclosure does not change what authors report or how complete reports become
- Main confounds: The case studies disclosure, not truthfulness of underlying self-report
- Evidence status: Preprint / accepted conference paper

## Sources collected

- `C030_P1` (preprint / open): AI Disclosure with DAISY — https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02760
