# Case 21: Synthetic respondents benchmarked against ANES/GSS/ESS

## Summary

Benchmark prompt-only personas, profile-rich personas, and calibrated hybrids against real survey descriptives and higher-order relationships.

## Why it matters for typology

It is the clearest case for splitting calibration from broader causal or transport claims.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: Synthetic panels recover higher-order relationships and heterogeneity as well as human data
- Main confounds: Human benchmark surveys are themselves noisy and historically contingent
- Evidence status: Peer-reviewed article
