# Case 2: Contested codebook measurement of manifestos, protest events, and political violence

## Summary

Compare AI-only, human-only, and hybrid codebook workflows on contested political-science constructs with hard negatives and transport tests.

## Why it matters for typology

This is the strongest direct test of a constitutive human role in social-science measurement.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: AI-only codebooks match human-revised codebooks on edge cases and transport
- Main confounds: Extra labels may be confounded with better construct work
- Evidence status: Peer-reviewed / preprint mix
