# Case 35: Doctoral-student AI use and training needs across dissertation workflows

## Summary

Use doctoral-student AI adoption patterns to identify where training shifts from prompt fluency to provenance, validation, and calibration are most needed.

## Why it matters for typology

Without a pedagogy case, the typology remains descriptive and misses role reproduction.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: Prompt-centric training performs as well as provenance- and validation-centric training expectations
- Main confounds: Self-reports may not match actual workflow traces
- Evidence status: Peer-reviewed article
