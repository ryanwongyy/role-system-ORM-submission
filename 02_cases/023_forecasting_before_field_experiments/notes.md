# Case 23: Forecasting outcomes before field experiments

## Summary

Inject AI forecasts into preregistration or design workflows and observe which design decisions actually change.

## Why it matters for typology

This is the most direct case for whether predictive AI changes design judgment rather than merely speeds it.

## Caveats

- Falsification test: Forecast-informed redesign improves calibration without narrowing novelty or explanatory ambition
- Main confounds: Extra deliberation time may drive gains rather than the forecast itself
- Evidence status: Preprint

## Sources collected

- `C023_P1` (preprint / open): Predicting Field Experiments with Large Language Models — https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.01167
