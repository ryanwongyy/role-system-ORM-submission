# Editorial-Reviewer Loop Plan

**Mission:** Prevent desk-rejection at *Organizational Research Methods* (ORM) across all grounds — scope fit, contribution clarity, methodological rigor, replication discipline, prose/voice. Fresh agent personas each iteration; no persona reuse.

**Baseline (v02):** Post-transplant, post-eye-pass, post-generic-editor-loop manuscript. 5,872 words, 180 .md lines, 5 tables.

**Convergence criteria:**
- Every persona says "would not desk-reject" 
- Mean editorial-fitness score ≥ 8.5 across all sections
- No critical structural issues flagged by any editor persona
- Diminishing returns: iteration changes fewer than 3 items

**Agent persona pool (each persona used at most once across the whole loop):**

Iteration 1 (G-personas, ORM editorial voices):
- G1: Editor-in-Chief / desk-rejection gatekeeper
- G2: Associate Editor for multi-method research
- G3: Associate Editor for quantitative methods
- G4: House copyeditor

Iteration 2 (H-personas, simulated reviewers):
- H1: Skeptical methodologist reviewer
- H2: Sympathetic theorist reviewer
- H3: Open-science / replication reviewer
- H4: External stylistic editor

Iteration 3 (I-personas, adjacent-paper perspectives):
- I1: Author-POV from Hickman et al. 2022 ORM
- I2: Author-POV from Aguinis et al. 2023 ORM
- I3: Editor who has rejected recent AI-in-research submissions
- I4: Prose final-pass editor

Iteration 4+ (J-, K-personas, held in reserve if convergence not reached).

**Output at convergence:** v0N_final.{md,docx} plus a desk-reject-risk memo.
